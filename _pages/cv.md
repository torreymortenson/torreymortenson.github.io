## Professional Experience

**Founder & Chief Experience Officer** — Civix Design, Idaho Falls, ID (2025–Present)
- Founded human-centered design consultancy focused on civic services and high-stakes public/private sector clients
- Services span service design, digital design, accessibility/ADA/WCAG compliance, AI integration and risk evaluation, human factors review, and process design
- [civixdesign.com](https://civixdesign.com)

**Human Factors Research Scientist** — Idaho National Laboratory / Battelle Energy Alliance, Idaho Falls, ID (2019–Present)
- Human reliability analysis, human factors, interface development and design, and data visualization research
- HRA for long-duration spaceflight; advanced control room analytics; deep learning and cognitive modeling for human error prediction; prognostic visualization for operator decision-making
- Developed and taught week-long intensive courses in HRA and human factors for the U.S. Nuclear Regulatory Commission
- Developing NRC guidance on Advanced Reactor Operator Licensing and human factors of remote/autonomous operations for advanced reactors
- Researching HCI aspects of explainable AI in critical infrastructure
- Supporting Nuclear Science and Technology directorate Research Operations leadership

**Adjunct Professor** — University of Idaho, Idaho Falls, ID (2022–Present)
- Graduate and undergraduate instruction in human performance, safety, and human factors
- Student research advising; new course development

**Materials and Fuels Complex Performance Analyst** — Idaho National Laboratory, Idaho Falls, ID (2018–2019)
- Supported Post-Irradiation Examination and Advanced Characterization divisions on Contractor Assurance principles and programs
- Developed human performance improvement (HPI) tools with conduct-of-operations leaders
- Developed and deployed management review meeting (MRM) template and process
- Cause analyst on emergent incidents, conditions, and fact findings
