A new course developed to integrate human-centered design principles and methodologies into nuclear engineering education. Students learn to apply HCD frameworks to the unique challenges of nuclear power plant design, operations, and safety.

**Topics include:** Human factors engineering, usability evaluation, user needs analysis, interface design principles, design review methods, human reliability considerations, and regulatory standards (NUREG-0700, NUREG-1791).

**Course development:** This course was developed from the ground up as part of an effort to integrate human factors and design into the nuclear engineering curriculum at the University of Idaho.

**Offered:** Spring 2021
