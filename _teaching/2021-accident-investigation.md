This course covers foundational and advanced methods in accident investigation with emphasis on human factors, organizational factors, and systems thinking. Students learn to apply structured investigation frameworks to real-world incidents in high-consequence industries.

**Topics include:** Root cause analysis, contributing factor analysis, causal modeling, human error taxonomy, event and causal factor charting, organizational safety culture, and regulatory reporting frameworks.

**Offered:** Fall 2021
