A field-oriented course in human performance investigation. Students develop practical skills in observing, analyzing, and reporting on human performance in organizational and operational settings.

**Topics include:** Human performance tools, observation methods, error precursor identification, performance influencing factors, field investigation techniques, and performance improvement planning.

**Offered:** Spring 2024, Spring 2026
